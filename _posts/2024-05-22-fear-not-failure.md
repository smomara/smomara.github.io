title: "Inactivity, not Failure: The True Obstacle to Success"
---

Time is our most precious resource. With every passing second, the past grows, the future shrinks, and our possibilities narrow. Inaction and missed opportunities accelerate this effect, but failure does not. Thus, the greatest risk we face isn't failure but the chances we don't take. Letting fear, doubt, or lack of confidence hold us back robs us of opportunity and compounds the effect of lost time.

The good news is that we have the power to create and seize opportunities. Often, the only thing standing between us and success is our belief in ourselves and our fear of failure. But, as mentioned before, failure isn't our biggest threat. You can pursue goals even if you're not fully qualified. Taking action, no matter how small, builds confidence. With every success and each challenge you face, your confidence grows stronger. You will be surprised by how successful a "bad" plan is when executed with determination. There is limitless upside in giving yourself a shot.
